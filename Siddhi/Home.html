Get to know us

Our website has many more purposes besides serving as a platform for booking turf. Our website offers services like team creation, player invitation, choosing the best playing surface, and many more. For instance, if your team consists of seven people and you want to play football, seven persons on the field is far too few for an entertaining match. However, you only know seven individuals and are unsure of where to find the other seven participants. We therefore spent some time on this before coming up with a concept. We provide two-team matches, so all you have to do is create your team, and the algorithm of the website will find or create your competitor team. We also provide a range of payment methods for the convenience of our clients.
